Maik Brockmann is Managing Partner at CERTINA GROUP. Maik has gained experience with two investor groups since 1998 and has been part of over 30 transactions in the SME and carve-out sectors.
From 1998 to 2006, he worked for Certina Holding AG in Munich and was responsible for the Operations division, and he was significantly involved in the transformation processes in the shareholdings within the Certina Group.
From 2006-2010, Maik was Vice President Operations at the listed Arques Industries AG, where he was subsequently appointed to the Executive Board. In this role, he transformed Arques Industrie AG into Gigaset AG as Chairman of the Board of Management and Chief Sales Officer and became one of the youngest Tec DAX board members.
Maik is currently responsible for the realignment of the companies as Managing Partner
