Tobias Eiblmeier is Head of M&A and Corporate Development at the CERTINA GROUP and is responsible for the group's national and international investment activities.
Tobias has more than 10 years of experience in the private equity industry in the areas of M&A as well as turnaround and transformation management. Prior to joining the CERTINA GROUP, he worked for many years at a listed private equity company with a focus on special situations, where he was fully responsible for the global buy-side and sell-side activities. As an active investor, he has also supported numerous medium-sized companies from various industries in their transformation.
Tobias holds a degree in Business Administration from the Munich University of Applied Sciences and a Master's degree from the Munich University of Applied Sciences.
