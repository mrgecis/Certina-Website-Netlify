András v. Kontz is Managing Partner at the CERTINA GROUP and responsible for the Goods & Services division.
András has many years of experience in the field of M&A and in the transformation of medium-sized companies.
Before joining the CERTINA GROUP, he worked for international private equity companies and financiers with a focus on special situations.
András v. Kontz holds a degree in International Management from ESCP Business School and a Master's degree from Cass Business School, London.
