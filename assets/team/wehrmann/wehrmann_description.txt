Hans Wehrmann is the founder, chairman of the board and majority shareholder of the CERTINA GROUP. Hans began his professional career in restructuring at the Boston Consulting Group. In 1998, he made his first company acquisition, laying the foundation for the CERTINA GROUP. Since its foundation, he has carried out more than 65 successful transactions and turnarounds and built the CERTINA GROUP into a solid and sustainable industrial holding company with an international orientation.
