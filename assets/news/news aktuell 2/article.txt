[TITLE]
Prof. Vladimír Hönig joins CERTINA Academy

[TEXT]
The CERTINA GROUP is delighted to welcome Assoc. Prof. Vladimír Hönig, Ph.D. et Ph.D. as its new Growth Partner within the CERTINA Academy. 

Vladimír will support the management in decisions for the direction of CERTINA GROUP's research and development, respond to current research challenges and participate in research and innovation projects. Thanks to his long-term experience in R&D, he will coordinate research activities in the areas of automotive, circular economy and decarbonisation. 

He is currently working at the Prague University of Economics and Business (CZ) and the Czech University of Life Sciences Prague (CZ), where he is researching advanced materials and technologies for the automotive industry and developing strategic frameworks for ministries and other state institutions in the field of decarbonisation.

He also serves on the advisory board of the Quality Council and the Centre for the Economics of Regulated Industries, where he applies his research experience to applications in European industry.

Prof. Häcker from the Certina Academy comments: “We are delighted that Prof. Hönig has agreed to join the Certina Academy. In Prof. Hönig, we have a proven expert in the automotive field who thinks beyond his own sector and is able to put things together holistically.”
