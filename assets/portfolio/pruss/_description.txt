Pruss, based in Hanover, Germany, specializes in manufacturing high-performance control valves for the energy and process industries. The company's products are designed to control flow, quantity, and pressure in demanding applications such as power plants and chemical processing. Pruss is recognized for its engineering depth and the ability to deliver customized valve solutions to clients such as Siemens, Alstom, and Babcock. Its control valves are essential for the safe and efficient operation of large-scale industrial systems.
