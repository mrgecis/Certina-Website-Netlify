WPS (WPS Parking Solutions) is a leading Dutch company offering innovative, cloud-based parking management systems. Specializing in open, adaptive solutions, WPS develops software, payment, and analytics systems that seamlessly integrate with hardware. With offices in the Netherlands, Belgium, UK, Canada, and the USA, WPS provides customized solutions for cities, airports, hospitals, and commercial clients worldwide. The company is known for its user-friendly technology, flexible architecture, and a strong focus on digital transformation in the parking sector.
