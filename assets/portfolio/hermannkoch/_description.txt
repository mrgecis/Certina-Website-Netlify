Hermann Koch, also known as HK Cosmetic Packaging, is a highly specialized producer of injection-molded plastic jars, sticks, and packaging systems for the cosmetics, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries. Located near Coburg, Germany, and with a heritage of more than 100 years, HK combines traditional craftsmanship with modern production technology. The company offers a wide variety of shapes, finishes, and decorations, delivering both standard and fully customized packaging solutions for international brands.
