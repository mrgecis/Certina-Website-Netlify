ENORISE specializes in software development and testing solutions for the mobility industry, focusing on next-generation electric and autonomous vehicles. Headquartered in Auburn Hills, USA, the company delivers advanced test bench systems and customized solutions that enable automotive manufacturers to develop, validate, and optimize powertrains and vehicle components for carbon-neutral mobility. ENORISE's portfolio also includes engineering services and simulation technologies, supporting clients from concept to series production in their transition to sustainable mobility.
