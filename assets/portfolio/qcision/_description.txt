Qcision (formerly Feintool Technologie AG Lyss) is a Swiss technology leader in fineblanking and forming systems, delivering high-precision presses for automated, high-volume metalworking. The company's machinery is used in the automotive, electronics, and consumer goods industries, where fine tolerance and repeatability are essential. Qcision also provides digitalization and automation solutions for Industry 4.0 production environments, helping clients to boost productivity and flexibility.
