Shieldtech Group designs and manufactures advanced thermal and acoustic shielding products, primarily for the automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors. With sites in Switzerland (Sevelen) and the USA (Buford), the company develops solutions that reduce heat and noise in engines, exhaust systems, and industrial equipment. Shieldtech's expertise includes high-temperature materials, lightweight construction, and customized engineering, enabling OEMs to meet stricter emissions and comfort standards.
