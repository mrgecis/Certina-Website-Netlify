Sustainability in Action

Selected ESG achievements from our portfolio companies

REBHAN

A leading manufacturer of innovative, high-quality plastic packaging with a focus on environmentally friendly products. REBHAN's Green Line is a sustainable approach to packaging based on the 5R principles: Reduce, Recycle, Reuse, Renew and Refill. The Colonna PET bottle saves 60% weight compared to a conventional bottle.

HORN

Specializes in design, manufacture and assembly of melting furnaces and special machinery for glass production. HORN is the world's leading manufacturer of hybrid furnaces, which incorporate electrical energy in addition to fossil fuels and thus contribute significantly to reducing carbon dioxide emissions.

UST

Europe's leading manufacturer of high-quality hairdressing scissors. By switching production from traditional hot forging to the cold forging process used in the automotive industry, UST was able to significantly reduce energy requirements in production and save raw materials.
